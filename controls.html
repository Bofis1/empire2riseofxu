Mouse Input

Mouse Controls

Left Click / Hold — Attack in cursor direction. Hold to auto-attack.
Right Click / Hold — Block. Reduces incoming damage. Depletes stamina over time.
Scroll Wheel — Adjust camera zoom distance.
Move Cursor — All skills and attacks aim at the mouse cursor position in world space.
Survival Guide

Tips for New Players

TIP 01

Don't ignore the Outpost. The crafting benches, merchants, and skill trainers in the XU Outpost can dramatically accelerate your power. Visit before each new zone push.

TIP 02

Combo multiplier is everything. At x10 combo, your damage is ten times base. Learn to dodge-weave attacks rather than tank hits — preserving your combo is worth more than the damage you avoid.

TIP 03

Parry timing. Block just as an enemy swings (watch their tell animation) to trigger a perfect parry. The counterattack deals 3× your ATK and staggers most enemies.

TIP 04

Socket your gems early. Press J to open the gem panel. Even common rubies in your weapon slots add meaningful ATK. Players who ignore socketing fall behind quickly.

TIP 05

Skills synergize. Don't just pick the highest-damage skills. Pair a gap closer (Leap Strike, Blink) with a burst skill (Execute, Nova) and a sustain tool (Heal Pulse, Fortify) for survivable aggression.

TIP 06

Boss phases telegraph their escalation. When a boss enters a new phase, there's a 2-3 second window before the new attack pattern begins. Use this to heal, reposition, and check your mana.

TIP 07

In multiplayer, zone entry matters. All enemies are server-side and persistent. If your party is in Patrol Zone but you're in the Outpost, enemies in Patrol Zone are still active — they will be there when you arrive.

TIP 08

Level 100 isn't the end. The Ascendancy System begins at the cap. Farm AA Points in endgame zones to unlock abilities that dramatically reshape your combat identity.
