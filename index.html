Core Systems

Built for Combat

🎥
Three Camera Modes

Play it your way. A single mouse wheel zooms through three perspectives — first-person, third-person, and the classic isometric overhead. Scroll in for first-person aim, out for the overhead view; you start in third-person. See the camera guide →

v93.0 CAMERA
∞
The Convergence

The endgame zone where reality cracked when Overseer Zero died. Procedural BSP layouts, infinite depth scaling, 13 modifiers stacking into cataclysms every 5 depths. The Depth Sentinel waits at every floor — kill it, take the magenta portal, go deeper. The deeper you go, the meaner it gets.

v93.0 ENDGAME
💎
Echo Shards & The Archivist

Convergence-only currency that persists across deaths. Spend at The Archivist in the Outpost — a 15-node meta-progression tree across three tiers. Tier I boosts you forever; Tier II buffs you only in the Convergence; Tier III is game-changing (Glass Cannon, Lucky Star, Boss Slayer, Reroll Token).

v93.0 META
💡
Lightstones — 11 Items, 10 Affixes

A 9th equipment slot, fully evolved. Eleven items from Tallow (common) to mythics like Aether Beacon (drops light puddles on damage), Aether Forge (AOE damage burst every 12s), Communal Lantern (scales with party size). Plus 10 lightstone-only affixes: Pulsing (orb throbs), Iridescent (rainbow shift), Burning (damages enemies in range). MP-synced — your party glows too.

v93.0 PHASE 2
⚡
Racial Active Skills

Every race now has a bespoke ACTIVE skill on Hotkey 9. Xu's Crest Flare fears enemies. Centaur's Charge sprints with +200% next-hit. Vampire's Void-Step teleports through walls. Gnome's Automaton fights beside you. Ogre's War Chant buffs the whole party. Minotaur's Vreth Stance reflects damage. Elf's Void-Sight reveals everything on the minimap. 32 total racial traits.

v93.0 RACES
👁️
The Depth Sentinel — Custom AI

The Convergence boss now has a bespoke kit designed around the lightstone system. 5 phases (Awakening → Fracture → Cascade → Collapse → Void), 5 attacks, 5 passives. Lightstone Echo damages you LESS if you're brightly lit. Light Drain feeds on your illumination. Darkness Curse temporarily disables your lightstone for 4s. Burning affix lightstones deal 3× damage to it — gear matters.

v93.0 BOSS
⚔️
Real-Time Combat

Click to attack, aim with mouse. Chain hits into x3, x6, x10 combo multipliers. Block, parry, counter with timing-based defense.

CORE
🌐
Multiplayer Co-op

Real-time WebRTC multiplayer. Create or join games from the lobby. Party up to 4. MMO-style persistent enemy state.

ONLINE
🧬
8 Races · 32 Traits · 4 Classes

Every race brings 4 hand-built traits — 3 powers and 1 weakness. Stacked with 4 class kits (Warrior, Mage, Rogue, Ranger), no two builds play the same.

BUILD
💎
Gem Socketing

Socket rubies, sapphires, emeralds, void gems into gear. 28 craftable items across weapon, armour and accessory benches.

CRAFTING
🌟
Ascendancy System

Hit level 100 and unlock powers beyond the cap. Shadow Step, Assassinate, Aether Surge, Iron Will and more.

ENDGAME
🌍
30 Zones

From the ruined Outpost to The Reach at the end of all things. Hand-crafted zones from Lv 5 fey gardens to Lv 100+ post-cap raids, plus the procedural Convergence, the golden Xu metropolis of Xulcan Prime, and the one-way red rift to the final zone.

WORLD
🏰
Guild System

Form guilds with up to 50 members. Real-time guild chat, MOTD, persistent guild XP. Level perks unlock at Lv 2/3/5/7: +20% XP, +15% gold, epic loot boost.

SOCIAL
🏆
Achievements & Titles

60+ achievements scaling from first kill to 100,000-kill empire slayer. Earn titles like "the Reaper" or "Apex Conqueror" — display them above your name.

PROGRESSION
📅
Daily Login Rewards

7-day cycling reward calendar. Gold, potions, gems, legendary loot. Build a streak — miss a day, it resets. Unlock the "Devoted" title at 30 days.

RETENTION
🌀
Apex Trials

Endgame portals open into 8-tier rifts with stacking modifiers — Burning, Frozen, Charged, Toxic, Swarm, Cursed, Mirrored. Higher tier = harder mods + better loot. Curator's Apprentice trades shards for tier-locked legendaries. Tier VIII (XERON tier) drops bonus shards.

v92.13–v92.73
👺
World Bosses

Six cross-zone roaming bosses — Forge Tyrant, Eyexor, Hollow Reaper, Void Behemoth, Abacus of Flesh, and the apex Overseer of Discord. Spawn every 5 minutes across the map. Drop tier-appropriate Apex Shards plus legendaries on kill; Overseer of Discord drops the new STORMFORGED set directly.

v92.20 · a156

Gold Vault

Death-proof savings. Deposit gold safely — die and lose 15% of your wallet, but vault gold survives. No fees. Per-character storage that persists through every wipe.

v92.25

Six-tier shard currency dropped from Apex Trials and World Bosses. Trade at the Curator's Apprentice for 25 dungeon-only legendaries — including the Lich Sovereign's Scepter and Frostfang of the Sovereign.

v91.1
⚒️
THE FORGE

The molten foundry where the Xu forged their war machines — a Lv 95 industrial hellscape of lava channels, conveyor lines and a central reactor arena. Four new foundry mobs (Molten Crawlers, Lava Forged Sentinels, Forge Technicians, Industrial Devastators) guard the approach to THE FURNACE CORE, a colossal weak-point boss. Drops the Obsidian Forge set — black volcanic glass veined with lava — plus the Cindercleaver, Magmabreaker, Pyrelith Scepter and Aegis of the Forge.

a366 NEW ZONE

The Blooming Wilds

A pre-Xu fey garden the war never reached. Lv 10+ entry zone accessed from north Patrol. Six new enemies — Bloom Sprites, Glimmer Fairies, Mushroom Brutes, Vine Stalkers, Pollen Wraiths, Thorn Knight mini-boss. The Wildmother sleeps at the heart, an ancient willow tree-being with vine arms and floating moonstones.

v92.49
⛪
The Veiled Sanctuary

Hidden raid accessed from the Necropolis. Lv 90+ recommended. A monastery where the Forsaken Order tried to seal a void rift and got frozen mid-prayer. Heavy purple-gold thunderstorm with sacred lightning. Drifting void tornadoes that damage on contact. Eight unique enemies + The Final Abbot at the heart, halo and twin censer-staves.

v92.37–v92.46

Quality of Life

Inventory panel is now draggable + resizable + persistent — move it anywhere, save layout per character. Drag potions onto the item bar (Z X C V G) or Shift+Right-click to auto-assign. All 9 ascendancy actives now functional with smart U + Shift+U binding.
